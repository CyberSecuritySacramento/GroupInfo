# Code of Conduct

We do not condone harassment against any participant, for any reason. Harassment includes deliberate intimidation and targeting individuals in a manner that makes them feel uncomfortable, unwelcome, or afraid.

Participants/Members asked to stop any harassing behavior are expected to comply immediately. 

We reserve the right to respond to harassment in the manner we deem appropriate, including but not limited to expulsion and referral to all relevant parties.


Inspired by the DEFCON [Code of Conduct](https://defcon.org/html/links/dc-code-of-conduct.html)
